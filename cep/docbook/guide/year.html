year

Name

year — The year of publication of a document

Synopsis

Mixed Content Model

year ::=
(#PCDATA|link|olink|ulink|emphasis|trademark|replaceable|remark|
 subscript|superscript|inlinegraphic|inlinemediaobject|indexterm)*

Attributes

Common attributes

Description

Year identifies a year. In DocBook V3.0, this is only used in Copyright, to identify the year or years in which copyright is asserted.

Processing expectations

Formatted inline.

Parents

These elements contain year: copyright.

Children

The following elements occur in year: emphasis, indexterm, inlinegraphic, inlinemediaobject, link, olink, remark, replaceable, subscript, superscript, trademark, ulink.

Examples

For examples, see bibliography, biblioset, bookinfo, copyright .
